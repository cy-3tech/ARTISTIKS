THE ARTIST

ArtiStiks is a rising force from KwaThema, whose music is rooted in real-life experiences and the untold stories of kasi life. Known for blending raw storytelling with authentic street emotion, he uses his sound to reflect the struggles, growth, and resilience of his upbringing.

In 2022, he made his mark by hosting the successful Hustle Fest, and continued building his presence with a standout appearance on Unplugged and Unreleased in 2024. His artistry is driven by a mission to give a voice to the realities often overlooked by the mainstream.

With his upcoming EP "Kasi Nova," ArtiStiks invites listeners into his world—one shaped by hustle, survival, and ambition—cementing his place as one of the voices to watch from the new wave of South African hip hop.
